Learn About SDG 12

What is SDG 12?

Sustainable Development Goal 12 aims to ensure sustainable consumption and production patterns. It focuses on reducing waste, promoting resource efficiency, and encouraging sustainable lifestyles.

Key Targets

  • Reduce food waste by 50% by 2030
  • Achieve sustainable management of natural resources
  • Reduce waste generation through prevention and recycling
  • Encourage companies to adopt sustainable practices

How You Can Help

  • Choose products with minimal packaging
  • Buy local and seasonal food
  • Use public transport or carpool
  • Reduce energy consumption at home
  • Recycle and compost waste
